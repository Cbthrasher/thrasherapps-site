StoreWatt Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 6, 2026

StoreWatt is built to work on battery storage sites with zero connectivity, so it is offline and private by design. We collect nothing. This policy explains exactly how your information is handled.

Information We Collect

None. StoreWatt has no account, no login, no servers, and no analytics or advertising SDKs. We never see your calculations, your site data, your forms, your photos, or anything else you do in the app.

Data Stored on Your Device

Company, crew, and site profiles, equipment specs, completed forms, scanned receipts, photos, signatures, and generated PDFs are stored only on your device inside the app's own container. Deleting the app deletes them. Nothing is synced, uploaded, or backed up by us anywhere.

Camera and Photos

StoreWatt asks for camera access only when you attach site photos to a form or scan a receipt. Receipt text reading (OCR) runs entirely on your device using Apple's Vision framework. Images are never transmitted anywhere by the app.

Email and Sharing

When you email or share a PDF, the app hands the document to Apple Mail or the iOS share sheet, addressed to recipients you configured. That send happens only when you tap it; documents queued while offline wait on your device until you choose to send them.

Networking

The only network connection the app itself makes is to Apple's App Store / StoreKit, to offer and verify the optional StoreWatt Pro purchase. Those transactions are handled entirely by Apple; we never receive your payment details.

Children's Privacy

StoreWatt does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we'll update the date above and post the revised policy here.
