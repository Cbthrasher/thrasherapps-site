What it does

Most cleaner apps trap you in weekly subscriptions and scare tactics. Winnow is the honest one.

Exact duplicates, similar shots from the same moment, blurry photos, screenshots, burst series, and your biggest videos.

In every group of lookalikes, Winnow badges the sharpest, highest resolution shot so you can clear the rest with one tap.
