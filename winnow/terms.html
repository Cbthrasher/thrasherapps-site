What Winnow Does

Winnow scans your photo library on your device and helps you review and remove items you choose. You are always in control: nothing is removed without your selection, your confirmation on the review screen, and the iOS system confirmation.

Deletion and Recovery

Items you remove are moved by iOS to the Recently Deleted album in the Photos app, where Apple keeps them recoverable for 30 days. After that period, the Photos app removes them permanently. Please review your selections carefully; you are responsible for what you choose to remove.
