What it does

Daily logging that takes seconds, insights that take you seriously.

🔥 One-tap hot flash tally

Tap once every time a hot flash hits. Log symptoms with quick chips and sliders — a full daily check-in takes under 30 seconds.

🔒 100% private

No account, no login, no ads, no tracking. Your intimate health data stays on your device — encrypted at rest, with optional Face ID lock.

📄 Doctor-ready reports

Generate a clean PDF of your symptom frequency, hot flashes, cycle history, and treatments — made to bring to your GP or gynecologist.

📊 See your patterns

A monthly heatmap shows heavier and lighter stretches at a glance, with trends for every symptom you track.

🩸 Irregular-cycle friendly

Perimenopause cycles are unpredictable. Log skipped and late periods easily and see your cycle length variability over time.

💊 Treatment tracking

Track HRT, supplements, and lifestyle changes — and see how your symptoms compared before and after starting each one.

Pause Meno is a personal tracking tool, not a medical device. It does not diagnose, treat, or provide medical advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional.
