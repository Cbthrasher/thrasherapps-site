What it does

One job, done well: capture an attack the moment it starts, when filling in a long form is the last thing you want to do.

⚡ One tap to log

Tap once and an attack starts now. Intensity, symptoms, triggers, relief, and the end time are all optional, added during the attack or after it passes.

🔒 100% private

Everything stays on your phone. No account, no login, no servers, no ads, no tracking. Your health data never leaves your device.

🩸 Cycle aware

Hormonal shifts are a common migraine trigger and most apps ignore them. Optionally factor menstrual cycle phase into your insights.

📊 Your triggers

Insights surface the patterns in your own logs: top triggers, frequency trends, average intensity and duration, and what relief worked best.

📄 Doctor ready reports

Export a clean PDF for any date range, in a full patient version or a focused physician summary built around headache day frequency and impact.

🌙 Easy on the eyes

A true low light theme for when you are photophobic, plus full Dynamic Type and VoiceOver support throughout.

Logging is always free, including your full history, the calendar heatmap, and your monthly stats. Pro adds Insights, cycle analysis, Apple Health correlations, doctor report PDFs, custom tags, and premium themes.

Privacy Terms Support

Head Pain Journal is a personal tracking tool, not a medical device. It does not diagnose, treat, or give medical advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional.
