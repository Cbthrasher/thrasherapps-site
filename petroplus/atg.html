Quick workflows for alarms, sensors, and test/status checks. Add exact menu paths as you confirm them.

Rule: Don’t guess. Capture evidence (screen photo + notes), then verify physically when applicable.

Workflow

Alarm triage

Steps
  1. Identify: active alarm vs history
  2. Identify: tank/device/sensor tied to alarm
  3. Capture evidence: photo of screen + time + what you saw
  4. Decide next step: verify condition vs sensor issue vs escalate
  5. Communicate to customer: what you know + what you’re checking next
