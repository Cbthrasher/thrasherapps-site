What it does

The hard part of invoicing isn't making one — it's chasing the unpaid ones. PaidUp is built for exactly that.

⏱️ Invoices in seconds

Add a client and line items with a running total as you type. Tax, discounts, deposits, and notes when you need them.

💸 Get paid your way

Put your own payment links — PayPal, Venmo, Cash App, Zelle, or bank details — plus a scannable QR code right on a polished PDF.

🔔 Never chase again

A "Money Owed" dashboard surfaces overdue invoices and nudges you to follow up — with polite, ready-to-send reminders.

🔒 100% private

No account, no login, no ads, no tracking. Everything stays on your device, and you can export or delete it all anytime.

📄 Professional PDFs

Your logo and accent color on a clean invoice, sent by email, Messages, or any app.

✅ Honestly priced

The free tier has no invoice limits. Pro adds automated reminders, branding, export, and more.

PaidUp is an invoicing and record-keeping tool. It is not tax, legal, or financial advice.
