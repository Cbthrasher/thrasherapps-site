TrialMate Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 2, 2025

Thrasher Apps LLC ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how TrialMate handles your information.

Information We Collect

TrialMate may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Information you voluntarily provide such as case details, witness names, and notes entered into the app.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous usage statistics to help us improve the app experience.
  • AI-Generated Content: When you use AI-powered features (question generation, argument generation, cross-examination prep), your input is sent to third-party AI services for processing.
  • iCloud Data: If you enable iCloud sync, your case data is stored in your personal iCloud account.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Operate and provide the app's features
  • Process AI-powered requests through third-party services
  • Sync your data across devices via iCloud
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Respond to support inquiries

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data in limited circumstances:

  • AI Service Providers: Case details you submit for AI-generated content are processed by third-party AI APIs (e.g., OpenAI). These providers have their own privacy policies.
  • Apple: Subscription and payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

Local and Cloud Storage

Your case data, witness information, notes, and documents are stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud sync, data is also stored in your personal iCloud account under your Apple ID. We do not have direct access to your iCloud data.

In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

TrialMate offers auto-renewable subscriptions processed by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple handles all payment processing according to their privacy policy.

Document Scanning

TrialMate includes a document scanner feature. Scanned documents are stored locally on your device and in iCloud if sync is enabled. Scanned content is not transmitted to external servers unless you use an AI-powered feature on that content.

Children's Privacy

TrialMate is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have inadvertently collected such data, we will delete it promptly.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of AI-powered features
  • Delete all app data by removing the app from your device

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes are effective immediately upon posting. The Effective Date above will be updated accordingly.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@thrasherapps.com
