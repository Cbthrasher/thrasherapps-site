StreakQuest Support

Get help with StreakQuest and find answers to common questions.

Getting Started

StreakQuest helps you build daily habits through streaks and rewards. When you first open the app, you will see default habits organized into four categories: Fitness, Learning, Productivity, and Wellness.

Tap the checkmark button next to any habit to check in for the day. Your streak count will increase each consecutive day you complete the habit.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do streaks work?

Check in on a habit every day to build your streak. If you miss a day, your streak resets to zero unless you have a streak freeze available.

What are streak freezes?

Streak freezes protect your streak when you miss a day. If you have a freeze available and miss a day, the freeze is automatically used and your streak is preserved. You can buy freezes with coins or purchase them directly in the store.

How do I earn coins?

You earn 5 coins each time you check in on a habit. Complete all your habits in one day for a 20 coin bonus. Reach 7-day streaks for 50 bonus coins and 30-day streaks for 200 bonus coins.

How do I add a new habit?

Tap the Add Habit button at the bottom of the home screen. Enter a name and select a category. You can also create custom categories for 100 coins.

How do I delete a habit?

Swipe left on any habit and tap Delete. You can also go to Manage Habits from the toolbar and delete habits there.

How do I reset my data?

Go to Profile and scroll to the bottom. Tap Reset All Data to clear all habits, coins, and progress.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes. All features work offline except for in-app purchases, which require an internet connection.
