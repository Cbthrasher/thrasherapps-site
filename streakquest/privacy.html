StreakQuest Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 25, 2025

StreakQuest is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

StreakQuest does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. All your habit data, preferences, and progress are stored locally on your device.

Local Data Storage

The following information is stored only on your device:

  • Habit names and categories you create
  • Streak counts and completion history
  • Coin balance and purchase history
  • App preferences and settings
  • Notification preferences

This data never leaves your device unless you choose to back it up through iCloud or iTunes.

In-App Purchases

When you make an in-app purchase, the transaction is processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple handles all payment processing according to their privacy policy.

We store a record of completed transaction IDs locally on your device to prevent duplicate fulfillment. This information is stored securely in the iOS Keychain and is not transmitted anywhere.

Analytics and Tracking

StreakQuest does not use any third-party analytics services. We do not track your behavior, location, or app usage patterns.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, your notification preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your device token or notification history.

Children's Privacy

StreakQuest does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not require any personal information to function.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all StreakQuest data by:

  • Using the Reset All Data option in the Profile tab
  • Deleting the app from your device

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the Last Updated date above.
