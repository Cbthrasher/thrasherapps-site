What it does

Practical tools for petroleum service work—no fluff, just the numbers you need on site.

🧮 Precise cut lengths

Enter your tank measurements and get exact upper, lower, and total drop tube lengths for OPW 71SO, Franklin Defender, and EMCO overfill valves.

Why it's accurate

Real 95% math

Shutoff is solved at 95% of tank volume—the way a horizontal tank actually fills—instead of a rough 95% of height. The OPW method matches the 71SO worksheet, and the geometry follows the Georgia EPD drop-tube approach.

📊 Tank volume charts

Build a calibration chart from tank diameter, length, and capacity in 1/8-inch increments to find the level you need.

🔎 Error-code library

Search dispenser and ATG codes for Gilbarco, Wayne, and Veeder-Root TLS-300/350/450/450 Plus/4i—with plain-English causes and troubleshooting.

📄 PDF reports & history

Export a clean calculation report to share or keep on file, and pull up past jobs from your saved history.

🌐 Six languages

English, Spanish, French, German, Hindi, and Chinese—so the whole crew can use it.
