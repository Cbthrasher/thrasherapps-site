Side Work Tax Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 21, 2026

Side Work Tax is built privacy-first. All of your data stays on your device. This policy explains exactly how your information is handled.

Information We Collect

None. Side Work Tax has no account, no login, no servers, and no analytics or advertising SDKs. We never see your income, expenses, or anything else you enter.

Where Your Data Lives

All income, expenses, income sources, settings, and quarterly payment records are stored only on your device using Apple's on-device database (SwiftData). The app does not transmit this data anywhere.

Networking

The only network connection the app makes is to Apple's App Store / StoreKit, to offer and verify the optional Pro purchase. These transactions are handled entirely by Apple; we never receive your payment details.

Notifications

If you turn on quarterly reminders, the app schedules local notifications on your device. No notification data leaves your device.

Data You Export

The optional Pro export feature creates CSV/PDF files on your device and shares them only where you choose, via the standard iOS share sheet.

Children's Privacy

Side Work Tax does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children.

Deleting Your Data

You can delete everything at any time from Settings → Delete all data, or by removing the app from your device.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we'll update the date above and post the revised policy here.
