What it does

One job, done well: answer “how much do I stash for taxes?” every time you get paid.

⏱️ Log income in seconds

A big number pad and one tap to save. Add a source (Etsy, Uber, clients) and a note if you want—or just enter the amount and move on.

🫙 The Jar

A calm home screen with one clear answer: set aside $X—about N% of your income—plus your next quarterly due date.

Two modes

Simple or Smart

Set aside a flat percentage, or use the Smart estimate that factors in self-employment tax, federal brackets, your state, and any day-job W-2 income.

📅 Quarterly made clear

See each estimated-tax quarter—income, suggested set-aside, and due date—and mark payments as paid so you always know where you stand.

🧾 Expenses & export

Track deductible expenses to lower your set-aside, and export clean CSV and PDF summaries at tax time. Pro

🔒 100% private

Everything stays on your device. No account, no login, no bank connections, no ads, and no analytics. Nothing you enter is ever sent anywhere.
