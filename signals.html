FAQ

What broker do I need?
Any forex broker that supports the listed pairs. We use OANDA internally, but signals work with any broker. You trade on your own account.
How are signals delivered?
Real-time alerts via Telegram with exact entry price, stop-loss, and take-profit. You'll also have a web dashboard showing all active and historical signals.
What's the minimum account size?
No minimum required. Our signals include percentage-based risk (1% per trade), so they scale to any account size. A $500+ account is recommended for comfortable position sizing.
Are these guaranteed profits?
No. Trading involves risk and past performance does not guarantee future results. Our strategies have a validated edge over hundreds of trades, but individual trades can and will lose. Never risk money you can't afford to lose.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. No contracts, no lock-in. Cancel your subscription anytime and you won't be charged again.
